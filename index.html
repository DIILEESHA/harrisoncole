About the Firm

A Firm Built on Integrity & Results

"Every business deserves legal counsel that thinks like a partner, not a vendor."

Founded by James W. Harrison in 1996, Harrison & Cole has become one of New York's most respected boutique corporate law firms. We serve entrepreneurs, executives, and board members navigating complex business decisions — mergers, capital raises, regulatory challenges, and everything in between.

Our attorneys combine Ivy League pedigrees with hard-won boardroom experience. We don't just advise. We advocate.
